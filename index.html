About

A sincere
passion for music

Jungbeen Lee is a pianist and a music teacher based in Melbourne. Holding a double degree in Bachelor of Music and Bachelor of Education (Honours) at Monash University, Jungbeen brings over 20 years of piano experience alongside extensive teaching practice.

Jungbeen has taught in a wide range of settings — private studios, primary schools, and secondary colleges — working with students of all ages and abilities, from complete beginners to those preparing for AMEB, ANZCA and VCE Music exams. Her current role at Port Melbourne Secondary College reflects her commitment to delivering structured, engaging lessons that support consistent progress and build confidence.

As a performer, Jungbeen has built a diverse and dynamic portfolio, highlighted by recognition as a finalist in the Australian Music Prize. She has performed on major stages as a member of the Victorian State Schools Spectacular Orchestra and collaborated with respected ensembles such as Inventi Ensemble and Team of Pianists. Her performance experience spans classical, jazz, funk, and contemporary genres, alongside her work as a live band keyboardist, allowing her to bring versatility, musicality, and confidence to every performance.

Policy

Lesson Information

Location & Schedule

Lessons are held in Heathmont, but home visits can be arranged in Eastern Melbourne or the CBD with additional fees. Schedule is flexible and can be arranged on weekdays or weekends to suit your availability.

Payment

Lesson fees are slightly less than the standard hourly minimum teaching rates (Victorian Music Teachers' Association, VMTA). Payment details will be confirmed upon booking. Please contact us for current pricing and available time slots. Bookings are confirmed once payment has been received.

Cancellation

Please notify at least 24 hours in advance to reschedule. Same-day cancellations cannot be refunded or rescheduled.

Free Consultation & Trial Lesson

A free 30-minute consultation and trial lesson is available before starting lessons. Get in touch to arrange yours today.
