About

A sincere
passion for music

Hello, I'm Jungbeen Lee — a pianist and music teacher based in Melbourne. I hold a Bachelor of Music and Bachelor of Education (Honours) from Monash University, and bring over 20 years of piano experience alongside extensive teaching practice.

I've taught in a wide range of settings — private studios, primary schools, and secondary colleges — working with students of all ages and abilities, from complete beginners to those preparing for AMEB and VCE Music Performance. I currently teach at Port Melbourne Secondary College.

As a performer, I've been a finalist in the Australian Music Prize, performed as a keyboardist in live bands, and collaborated in ensembles and choirs.

Teaching Approach

At May 9, teaching is centred on individualised learning — ensuring each student feels supported, motivated, and appropriately challenged. Strong emphasis is placed on technical foundations, musical expression, and effective practice habits, while keeping lessons enjoyable and meaningful.
